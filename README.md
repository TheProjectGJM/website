## Who is LeBron James?

LeBron James is a professional basketball player widely considered one of the greatest NBA players of all time. Born on December 30, 1984 in Akron, Ohio, he has played for the Cleveland Cavaliers, Miami Heat, and Los Angeles Lakers throughout his career. James has won four NBA championships, four NBA MVP awards, and is the NBA's all-time leading scorer. He is known for his versatility, athleticism, and longevity in the league.
